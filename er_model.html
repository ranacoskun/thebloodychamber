Conceptual Map and E/R Model

Upon identifying 10 relevant items associated with the phenomenon “The Bloody Chamber”, the subsequent objective was to develop a conceptual map that would effectively illustrate our idea and its interrelations, while also providing a preliminary description of the selected objects. Throughout this phase, we carefully adhered to provided metadata, and used it to describe our items in natural language. The outcome of this process is an initial conceptual map that presents our items, accentuates the main characteristics associated with out topic and their relations.

Utilizing the initial conceptual map as our starting point, we proceeded to formulate the first Entity-Relationship (E/R) model. We summarized the overarching connections between the objects, delineating each triplet to comprehend how we could formalize our network. To achieve this goal, we defined the objects as a collection of abstract entities. This allowed us to more accurately specify possible linkages between the entities and attributes, abstracting our perspective to a more comprehensive level.
